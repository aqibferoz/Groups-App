To Have
In Finnish, expressing the possession is not done by using a specific verb. Instead you need to put the subject in the adessive case and use the verb olla at the 3rd person singular. Literally, it would mean something like "on me there is" but do not consider this too much.

-lla olla (to have) - present tense

-lla olla is also used in some unexpected example for English speakers :

Minulla on nälkä   -   I am hungry
Sinulla on kylmä   -   You are cold
Hänellä on jano   -   He/she is thirsty
