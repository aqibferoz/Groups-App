Group 4
How to know if a verb belongs to Verbityyppi 4 ?


It groups all the verbs ending in the following way : -Vta/-Vtä. However, ~80% of the verbs ending in -ita/itä and -eta/etä are part of the 5th and 6th group

Explanation

Following is a conjugation example with the verb haluta (to want) :

Present tense: drop the t + add the termination . For 3rd pers. sing.: add a or ä
