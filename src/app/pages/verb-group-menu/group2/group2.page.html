How to know if a verb belongs to Verbityyppi 2 ?


It groups all the verbs ending in the following way : -da/-dä

Explanation

Following is a conjugation example with the verb syöda (to eat) :

Present tense: drop the da/dä + add the termination . For 3rd pers. sing.: keep the stem
